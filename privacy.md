# Privacy Policy

2M2 Data Services, LLC (“2M2,” “we,” “us”) operates this website to provide general information about our company.

## Information we collect

- **Information you provide:** If you contact us by email, we may receive your name, email address, and the contents of your message.

## How we use information

We use information to:

- Respond to inquiries
- Maintain, secure, and improve the website

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the website (such as hosting and analytics), or when required by law.

## Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

## Your choices

You may contact us to request access to or deletion of personal information you have provided, subject to applicable law.
